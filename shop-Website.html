Our Local Story

Neighborhood Market has been serving the Localville community since 1985. What started as a small family-owned grocery store has grown into a community hub where neighbors meet and local producers showcase their best products.

We're committed to supporting our local economy by sourcing over 60% of our products from within 50 miles of our store. From farm-fresh produce to artisan baked goods, we take pride in offering the highest quality items while keeping our carbon footprint small.

Stop by and experience the difference that shopping local makes. Our friendly staff is always here to help you find exactly what you need or make suggestions for your next meal.
